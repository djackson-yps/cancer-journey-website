Your privacy matters deeply. This app was built to support you through a personal and sensitive time. Your data belongs to you and is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than running the app.

Who We Are

My Cancer Journey is developed and operated by My Cancer Journey Limited, a company registered in England and Wales (company number 17318970). This is a personal health companion app designed to help individuals and their loved ones manage information during a cancer journey.

For data protection purposes, My Cancer Journey Limited acts as the Data Controller under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

What Data We Collect

The app collects and stores the following information that you choose to enter:

  • Account information — your name and email address used to sign in
  • Food diary entries — meals, nutritional data, and photos of food
  • Journal entries — personal diary entries and thoughts
  • Medical diary — appointments, dates, and notes
  • Medications — medication names, doses, and schedules
  • Documents — medical documents and files you upload
  • Notes — personal notes you create within the app
  • Music preferences — saved playlist links
  • Resource links — links to websites you save
  • App settings — your preferences such as access passwords and visible pages

We do not collect any data passively. Everything stored is data you have explicitly entered into the app.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Supabase stores data on servers provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS). Data may be held in data centres within the United States or European Union.

Supabase is compliant with SOC 2 Type II and provides encryption at rest and in transit for all stored data. Your data is protected by database-level access controls (Row Level Security) ensuring only authorised users can access it.

Uploaded documents and food photos are stored in Supabase Storage, a secure file storage service with access controlled by authentication tokens.

AI Processing

The app uses artificial intelligence features provided by Anthropic (claude.ai) for the following optional features:

  • Analysing photos of food to estimate nutritional content
  • Looking up nutritional information for foods you describe
  • Correcting grammar and spelling in journal entries
  • Improving clarity of journal entries
  • Looking up medication information

When you use these features, the relevant text or image is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not send any identifying information alongside AI requests. Anthropic's API data is not used to train AI models.

All AI features are optional. The app functions fully without using them.

Who Can Access Your Data

By default, only you can access your data. The app allows you to invite trusted people such as family members or carers with different levels of access:

  • Viewers — can read content you choose to make visible to them
  • Contributors — can add content such as notes and diary entries

You control who is invited and can revoke access at any time from Settings. Invited users cannot access your private journal entries or documents you have locked.

David Jackson, as developer, has administrative access to the Supabase database for maintenance and support purposes only, and does not routinely access user data.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Items moved to Recently Deleted are permanently removed after 30 days. Invited user accounts with no activity are automatically removed after 37 days.

If you close your account, all personal data — including health records, diary entries, medications, documents, food logs, appointments, and photos — will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Supabase infrastructure backups may retain data for up to 30 additional days before full purge.

You can request account deletion at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete My Account) or by emailing support@mycancerjourney.co.uk.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — you can object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Sensitive Health Data

This app handles sensitive personal health information including medical documents, medications, and health-related diary entries. This data is classified as special category data under UK GDPR.

We process this data solely on the basis of your explicit consent, which you give by choosing to enter this information into the app. This data is never shared with third parties except as described in this policy, and is never used for advertising or commercial purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

This app does not use advertising cookies or tracking technologies. We do not use analytics services such as Google Analytics. No data is shared with advertisers.

Supabase uses authentication tokens stored locally on your device to keep you signed in. These are essential for the app to function and are not used for tracking.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through this app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

ICO Registration

As a Data Controller processing personal data in the UK, we are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Our ICO registration number will be displayed here once registration is confirmed. You can verify registration at ico.org.uk.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint or by calling 0303 123 1113.

Data Breach Procedure

We take data security seriously. In the unlikely event of a personal data breach, we will:

  • Assess the breach within 24 hours of becoming aware of it
  • Report any breach likely to result in a risk to individuals to the ICO within 72 hours, as required under UK GDPR Article 33
  • Notify affected users without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms
  • Document all breaches, including those not reported to the ICO, in our internal breach register

If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at support@mycancerjourney.co.uk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@mycancerjourney.co.uk.
