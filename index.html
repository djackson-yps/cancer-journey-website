Why I built this

Built from personal experience

When I was diagnosed with cancer, I found myself juggling appointments, medications, a changing diet, mountains of paperwork, and trying to process it all emotionally — all at the same time. I couldn't find a single app that understood what that felt like.

So I built one. My Cancer Journey was created not in a boardroom, but from lived experience. Every feature exists because I needed it — or because someone I care about did. This is for you, built with love.

— David Jackson, Founder

Features

Everything in one place, always at your side

From what you eat to how you feel — My Cancer Journey keeps everything organised so you can focus on what matters most.

🍽️

Smart Food Diary

Just take a photo of your meal. Our AI recognises the food and logs estimated calories, protein, fat, and carbs automatically. No typing required.

📔

Personal Journal

Write how you're really feeling. The journal has AI tools to fix grammar and improve clarity — so your words always come out exactly as you mean them.

🎤

Voice Input

Not up to typing? Tap Voice and speak your thoughts. Your words are transcribed instantly so you can capture how you feel in the moment.

📅

Medical Diary

Keep track of every appointment, scan, and treatment date. Export to your phone's calendar or share a PDF with your care team.

💊

Medication Tracker

Log all your medications with doses and schedules. Never lose track of what you're taking, and look up information about any medication instantly.

📄

Medical Documents

Upload and store your letters, scan results, and important documents securely. They're always with you when you need them most.

🎵

Music Therapy

Save your Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube playlists in one place. Music is medicine — your favourites are always one tap away.

💜

Motivation Button

On the hard days, tap Motivate Me for something warm, uplifting, and personal. Because sometimes you just need a reminder of how strong you are.

🔗

Resource Library

Save links to charities, support groups, helpful websites, and anything else you want to revisit — all in one organised place.

Built for everyone around you

A cancer journey is never faced alone

When someone is going through cancer, everyone around them wants to help — family, friends, and the clinical team. But staying connected and informed without being intrusive is hard.

My Cancer Journey solves this. The person at the centre stays in full control, while the people supporting them — loved ones and healthcare professionals alike — can stay connected, contribute, and monitor progress, all within the same secure app.

For Healthcare Professionals

Monitor multiple patients from one account

My Cancer Journey isn't just for patients and families. Healthcare professionals — oncologists, doctors, nutritionists, and therapists — can be invited to multiple patients' journeys and switch between them seamlessly from a single login.

🩺

Oncologists & Doctors

Get invited as a Viewer to monitor a patient's appointments, medications, food diary and documents between clinical visits — without waiting for them to report back.

🥗

Nutritionists & Dietitians

Join as a Contributor to monitor a patient's food diary in real time. Track nutritional intake, flag concerns, and add notes — all within the patient's own app.

🧠

Therapists & Counsellors

Be invited as a Contributor to follow journal entries and mood notes. Stay connected to your patient's emotional journey between sessions.

👥

Multi-patient Access

Looking after more than one patient? Each patient invites you individually and you switch between their journeys from a single account — no separate logins needed.

🔒

Patient-controlled Privacy

The patient decides exactly what you can see. They assign your role, choose which sections are visible, and can revoke your access at any time.

📲

Works on Any Device

No app to install — just open the link on your phone, tablet or desktop. Access patient journeys wherever you are, whenever you need to.
